# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-18

Dominoyar is a domino scorekeeping app for web, PWA, and Android.

## Data Used By The App

Depending on how you use the app, Dominoyar may process:

- Account information used for sign-in.
- Profile settings you choose to save.
- Game progress and scorekeeping data.
- Device/app context needed for app operation, session continuity, troubleshooting, and security.

## Public Repository

This repository is public and is used for product information, release notes, support, and issue tracking.

Do not post private account details, passwords, tokens, personal contact information, or sensitive screenshots in public issues.

## Contact

Open a GitHub issue in this repository for public support questions.
